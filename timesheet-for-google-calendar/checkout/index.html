Questions

How do I receive my license key?
After payment, you'll be redirected to a confirmation page. Your license key will be emailed to you, usually within a few hours. Paste it in the add-on sidebar under Settings to activate Pro.
Can I use the key on multiple spreadsheets?
Yes. Your license is tied to your Google account, not a specific spreadsheet. Once activated, Pro works across all your spreadsheets.
What happens when my subscription renews?
You'll receive a new license key by email each year. Just paste the new key to extend your Pro access.
What if I'm not satisfied?
Full refund within 14 days, no questions asked. Email support-calendartimesheet@googlegroups.com.
How do I cancel?
You can cancel anytime from the subscription management page or by emailing support. Your Pro access continues until the end of the billing period.
