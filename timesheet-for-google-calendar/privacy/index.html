Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

The short version: Timesheet for Google Calendar reads your calendar events to generate timesheets in your spreadsheet. Your data never leaves Google's servers. We don't collect, store, transmit, or sell your data. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics.

1. What the Add-on Does

Timesheet for Google Calendar is a Google Sheets™ add-on that reads events from your Google Calendar™ and generates formatted billing timesheets directly in your active spreadsheet. All processing happens within Google's infrastructure using Google Apps Script.

2. Data We Access

The add-on requests the following Google API permissions (OAuth scopes):

  • calendar.readonly — Read-only access to your Google Calendar™ events, including event titles, start/end times, durations, event colors, descriptions, and your calendar list. The add-on cannot create, modify, or delete calendar events.
  • spreadsheets.currentonly — Write access limited to the currently active Google Sheets™ spreadsheet. The add-on creates formatted timesheet tabs in this spreadsheet only.
  • script.container.ui — Permission to display the configuration sidebar within Google Sheets™.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Calendar event data (titles, times, colors, descriptions) is read solely to generate your timesheet. Specifically:

  • Event titles are used to display event names and apply keyword-based project categorization
  • Start and end times are used to calculate durations and billing amounts
  • Event colors (colorId) are used to auto-categorize events into projects
  • Event descriptions are parsed for optional notes fields
  • Attendee response status is checked only to skip declined events

All this processing happens in real-time when you click "Generate Timesheet." No calendar data is cached, stored, or retained after generation.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to:

  • Your calendar events or event content
  • Your spreadsheet data
  • Your Google account information
  • Your email address or personal information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Any data whatsoever outside of Google's infrastructure

5. Data Storage

Your project mappings (color-to-project assignments, keyword rules, and hourly rates) are stored using Google Apps Script's PropertiesService, which keeps data within Google's infrastructure tied to your specific spreadsheet or user account. The developer (BrightDesk) has no access to this stored configuration data.

6. Third-Party Services

Timesheet for Google Calendar uses zero external APIs or third-party services. All code runs entirely within Google Apps Script. No data is transmitted outside of Google's servers at any point.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with any third party. There is no data to share — we don't collect any.

8. Children's Privacy

Timesheet for Google Calendar is a professional billing tool not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date. Material changes will be noted at the top of the policy.
